OUR STORY

Engineering the Future

Black Rank stands at the forefront of engineering and industrial innovation, delivering world-class EPC, O&M, and smart solutions across the energy, infrastructure, heavy industries, and digital transformation sectors.

Founded in 2011 as Integrated Systems and rebranded in 2025, Black Rank reflects a bold vision to redefine industrial excellence and sustainable growth across the MENA region and global markets. With over 14 years of regional expertise, strong global partnerships, and a commitment to state-of-the-art technologies, Black Rank consistently delivers value-driven, future-proof solutions tailored to meet the evolving demands of the modern world.
